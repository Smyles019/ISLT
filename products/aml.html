Introduction to Anti-Money Laundering System

In the banking industry, managing financial crime risks is crucial to maintaining trust and compliance.

Reg-Fusion AML solution is specifically designed to help financial institutions(Banks, insurance companies, Saccos and MFIs) comply with FRC (Financial Reporting Council), CBK (Central Bank of Kenya), and other regulatory requirements.

With our solution, financial institutions can ensure compliance, enhance security, and safeguard their business from money laundering and fraud risks.

Why RegFusion AML?

  • Customizable Rule Engine : Reg-Fusion AML rule engine is highly customizable, allowing banks to tailor alerts and thresholds according to their specific risks.
  • Scalable Solution : Reg-Fusion AML solution is designed to scale with your banking operations, making it capable of handling growing customer bases and more complex regulatory requirements as your bank expands.
  • Ensure Compliance with FRC, CBK, and Other Regulatory Requirements : Ensures that your bank avoids penalties and reputational damage.

Key Features for the Banking Industry

  • KYC (Know Your Customer): Ensures compliance during client onboarding by screening against sanction lists, adverse media, and Politically Exposed Persons (PEP) databases.
  • Sanction Screening: Regularly screens client data against updated sanction lists, using risk assessments to flag high-risk individuals.
  • Transaction Monitoring: Monitors customer transactions at regular intervals to detect unusual activities.
  • Case Management: Centralized system for managing investigations, transactions, alerts, and customer information.
  • Reports: Automates the creation and submission of compliance reports, including STRs, CTRs, and SARs.

Key Benefits

Risk Reduction: Helps mitigate financial and reputational risks through continuous monitoring and regular screenings.

Regulatory Compliance: Assures adherence to legal standards and requirements set by financial regulators.

Efficiency: Automates manual tasks like screening and reporting

Monitoring: Helps banks detect fraudulent activities or suspicious transactions in real-time, enabling swift action.
