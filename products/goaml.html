GoAML is an advanced reporting solution developed by ISTL to help financial institutions generate mandatory reports required by the Financial Reporting Centre (FRC) and the United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC).

This tool automates the generation of reports in a standardized XML format, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.

Features

  • Comprehensive Reporting : GoAML offers a powerful 3-in-1 package for monitoring and reporting on key performance metrics, including Currency Transaction Reports (CTR), Suspicious Transaction Reports (STR), and Suspicious Activity Reports (SAR).
  • Efficient Data Collection : GoAML seamlessly fetches data from core sources through secure DB-links or file-based methods, ensuring accurate and reliable information for report generation.
  • Automated Compliance Reporting : The solution ensures full compliance with FRC and UNODC requirements by providing standardized XML reports that facilitate data consistency and reporting accuracy.
  • Enhanced Monitoring and Detection : GoAML enhances the ability to monitor and detect cash transactions, suspicious transactions, and suspicious accounts, empowering institutions to identify and report potentially fraudulent or illegal activities with ease.

Key Benefits

Streamlined Reporting: Automates the process of generating regulatory reports.

Regulatory Compliance: Ensures that your reports meet FRC and UNODC standards

Operational Efficiency: Optimizes the data collection and report generation processes.

Real-time Monitoring: Improves the detection and reporting of suspicious activities.
